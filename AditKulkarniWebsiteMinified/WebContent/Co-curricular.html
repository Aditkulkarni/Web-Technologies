Sports and Association with charitable organizations

Apart from my passion for the Computer Sciences,another sphere that enthralled me was swimming.I was actively involved in competitive swimming coaching at Pegasus Health club,Pune and I was able to win medals and certifications for my city and state.I participated in many State Level and District Level Swimming competitions and won many of them.I have also played the Water-Polo on District and State level and our team won a few inter-city tournaments.Touched by the tragedies that befell new swimmers and lack of security at swimming pools,I decided to enroll my-self for ‘Live Saving Program’.This program was conducted by the International Life Saving Society and I became a certified life saver at a very young age and took oath of rescuing any individual in danger not just in water but on land too.I am adept at EAR(Expired Air Resuscitation)-CPR(Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation);techniques to revive people with respiratory arrest.EAR and CPR can provide oxygen to the body and may restore heartbeat and breathing.Being a certified Live Saver from the Royal Life Saving Society branch of International Life Saving Society gives me the joy of being a socially responsible citizen and has enthused great confidence within me.I am also involved in a few Rotary International activities as my father is a Rotary Member,and it offers me opportunities to volunteer in various Rotary’s Social Cause Projects.I also participated in various Blood Donations camps being conducted at our college or schools by various such institutions.
